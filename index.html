About

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at MTI Thrissur is a vibrant hub where innovation meets aspiration. As a student-driven initiative operating under the CET Centre for Interdisciplinary Research (CCIR), IEDC is committed to cultivating an entrepreneurial spirit among our students.

In close collaboration with esteemed partners like Kerala Startup Mission and CET TBI, IEDC serves as a catalyst for connecting students with industry insights and opportunities.

Our mission is to create an ecosystem that inspires, nurtures, and supports the innovative endeavors of our students.

About

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) at MTI Thrissur is a vibrant hub where innovation meets aspiration. As a student-driven initiative, IEDC is committed to cultivating an entrepreneurial spirit among our students.

In close collaboration with esteemed partners like Kerala Startup Mission, IEDC serves as a catalyst for connecting students with industry insights and opportunities.

Our mission is to create an ecosystem that inspires, nurtures, and supports the innovative endeavors of our students.
